About

Hi, I’m Igor — an indie game developer and C# programmer with 2 years of experience, primarily focused on the Unity engine. I’m currently in my final year of a Bachelor’s degree in Game Development at Collegium Da Vinci in Poznań. Aside from working with Unity, I enjoy creating cool and useful apps using the .NET ecosystem and all its goodies.

Projects

PhotoGraf

A fast-paced 3D game developed for the RE-use Game Jam. You play as a street photographer racing against time to pay overdue rent. Snap as many photos as possible before the clock runs out—while dodging traffic and staying alive on the bustling streets.

Abyssal Tides

A puzzle-driven 2D adventure where you control a clever octopus escaping a wrecked ship. Explore multiple levels filled with mini-games, tricky hazards, and collectible items. Manage eight inventory slots—but beware: lose a tentacle, and your storage shrinks while your movement slows, adding a tense survival twist.
