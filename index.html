About

Hi, I’m Igor — an indie game developer and C# programmer with 2 years of experience, primarily focused on the Unity engine. I’m currently in my final year of a Bachelor’s degree in Game Development at Collegium Da Vinci in Poznań. Aside from working with Unity, I enjoy creating cool and useful apps using the .NET ecosystem and all its goodies. Glad you stopped by!

Projects

PhotoGraf

A 3D game created during the RE-use Game Jam, in which you play as a street photographer who is late with paying your apartment rent. To earn money, you must take as many photos as possible in a short time while avoiding oncoming cars.

Abyssal Tides

A 2D game where you play as a small octopus trying to escape from a crashed ship. The game features several levels, each with a variety of puzzles that you must solve by playing mini-games, navigating hazards, and collecting useful items. The player has eight inventory slots for items, but if the octopus loses a tentacle, the space shrinks and the player's movements slow down.
