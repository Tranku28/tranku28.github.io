Abyssal Tides

Description:

A puzzle-driven 2D adventure where you control a clever octopus escaping a wrecked ship. Explore multiple levels filled with mini-games, tricky hazards, and collectible items. Manage eight inventory slots—but beware: lose a tentacle, and your storage shrinks while your movement slows, adding a tense survival twist.

Team size:

4

I was responsible for:
  • Movement mechanics and physics
  • In-game interactions
  • Inventory system
  • Minigames implementation
  • Audio programming
  • In-game events
